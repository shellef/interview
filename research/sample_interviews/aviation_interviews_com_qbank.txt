=== ABX AIR ===
1. Tell us about yourself.
2. What was the most difficult decision you made as a pilot?
3. Tell me about a time you discovered a safety issue and what did you do about it?
   Sample: "Talked about finding tires with tread worn down on a preflight walk around. Explained my process of reporting..."
4. How do you stay organized? Sample: "I make lists..."
5. How do you prevent procrastinating? Sample: "Set goals and work towards achieving them" / "Organization, writing down goals for the day"
6. What do you know about ABX?
7. Why ABX?
8. What are your favorite things about your current job? Sample: "Flexibility"
9. What are your least favorite things about your current job? Sample: "Unstable income"
10. Tell me about a time you had to quickly build a rapport with somebody.

=== AIR CANADA ===
1. How did you get interested in aviation? Sample: "family history"
2. What would your leadership style be as a captain?
3. Describe a problem/dispute that you had with a colleague?
4. How would your former supervisors describe you?
5. Describe your most memorable flying experience? Sample: "First solo"
6. How would you feel about being a relief pilot?
7. What is the greatest achievement in your career to date?
8. What was the hardest decision you've had to make in your career?
9. What do you dislike about your present job?
10. How did you deal with stress? Sample: "Preparing well ahead" / "First, address the stress; second, analyze the source and plan..."

=== AIR CARGO CARRIERS ===
1. Where do you see yourself in 5 years? Sample: "Check Airman" / "Instructor Captain"
2. What do you think defines a professional pilot? Sample: "Someone who is knowledgeable, punctual, able to communicate effectively"
3. What would you do if you lost your medical? Sample: "Request a ground instructor position"
4. What is V1? Sample: "Takeoff decision speed"
5. What is V2? Sample: "Takeoff safety speed"
6. What is a stopway? Sample: "Area at the end of the runway used for deceleration during aborted takeoff, not credited for takeoff roll"
7. Would you help load the airplane with boxes if ground crew is short-handed? Sample: "Of course"
8. Where do you see yourself in 10 years? Sample: "Chief Pilot"
9. What would you do if you witnessed someone sexually harassing someone else? Sample: "I would intervene, try to defuse the situation, report it"
10. What would you do if a passenger confronted you for going around? Sample: "Explain the safety decision professionally"

=== AIR TRANSPORT INTERNATIONAL ===
1. On an IFR flight in VFR, who is responsible for separation, you or ATC? Sample: "You — pilot in command"
2. When is a report to ATC required? Sample: "MARVELOUS CVFR 500 — leaving assigned altitude, entering/leaving holding, missed approach"
3. What is an LDA? Sample: "A non-precision approach offset from the runway using an ILS localizer"
4. What are you looking for in an employer/airline? Sample: "Team that functions like a family" / "Stability, great culture"
5. What is the correlation between temp and lightning strikes? Sample: "Higher temp = greater convective energy = more lightning"
6. What is the effect of aft CG on performance? Sample: "Rearward CG gives better performance but reduces stability"
7. What is Mach Tuck? Sample: "A nose down pitch tendency due to shift in center of pressure at high Mach"
8. What factors are used in calculating performance? Sample: "Temperature, humidity, power available, weight, runway condition"
9. What is critical Mach? Sample: "The lowest Mach number at which airflow reaches sound speed anywhere on the aircraft"
10. How did you get into flying?

=== AIR WISCONSIN ===
1. What is max airspeed in Class C? Sample: "200 knots indicated within 4 nautical miles"
2. At what altitude does the speed restriction end in Class B airspace? Sample: "10,000 MSL"
3. What is needed to descend below DA? Sample: "Required visibility and one of 91.175 visual references; must be in position for normal landing"
4. If flying 121, can you take off when weather is below landing minimums? Sample: "Yes, but you need to file an alternate"
5. What is BR on a METAR? Sample: "Mist"
6. How would your current boss describe you? Sample: "Dedicated team player" / "Safety oriented" / "Diligent"
7. What makes a good airline pilot? Sample: "Safety first, ability to focus, strong CRM, professionalism"
8. What will be your biggest challenge in training? Sample: "Learning the line" / "Adapting to crew environment after single-pilot ops"
9. Why do you want to work for Air Wisconsin? Sample: "Structured training, good reputation, stable employment"
10. What is your leadership style? Sample: "Lead by example" / "Collaborative but decisive"

=== COMMON TECHNICAL QUESTIONS ACROSS AIRLINES ===

Aircraft Knowledge:
- What is V1/VR/V2/VREF?
- What happens to stall speed as weight increases? (Increases)
- What happens to TAS as we climb at constant IAS? (Increases)
- What happens to Mach number as we descend at constant TAS? (Increases)
- Explain the coffin corner
- What is Dutch roll and how do you recover?
- Describe your aircraft's pressurization system
- What is a constant speed propeller and how does it work?

Weather/Met:
- What is a microburst and how do you escape?
- What is BR/FG/TS/SN on a METAR?
- Describe a cold front vs warm front
- What is clear air turbulence?
- What is a SIGMET vs AIRMET?

Regulations:
- Part 91 vs 121 vs 135 — key differences
- What are your currency requirements?
- Minimum equipment list — can you fly with inoperative equipment?
- What are the alternate minimums?
- Rest requirements under Part 117

Navigation:
- What is a VOR and how does it work?
- What is GPS RAIM?
- Describe an RNAV/RNP approach
- What is a hold entry (parallel, teardrop, direct)?
- DME arc — how do you fly one?
